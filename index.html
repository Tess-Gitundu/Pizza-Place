About Us

La pizzeria was founded in Italy by Adelasia Rossi who then brought her over-fired goodness in Kenya in the year 2001. Since then we've been baking the most fresh, delicious, cheesy, and juiciest pizzas in the country. Our branches are spread out across the country and can be accessed from any where in Kenya.
